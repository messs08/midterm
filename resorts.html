Playa Tropical

Playa Tropical Resort Hotel offers numerous on-site facilities to satisfy even the most discerning guest. Never be out of touch with your contacts, with free Wi-Fi offered throughout your stay. If you need a ride from or to the airport, the resort can arrange it before your check-in date. Exploring Ilocos Norte is made even more convenient with the car hire and shuttle services available at the resort.

If you plan to arrive by car, you'll appreciate the resort's free parking, right on-site. The resort provides front desk services such as concierge service and luggage storage for guests' convenience. If you need, the ticket service and tours can even help you book tickets and reservations at all the best shows and programs nearby. Staying for a long time, or just need clean clothes? At the resort, your favorite travel outfits will be kept clean and available with laundry service provided on-site.

Pangil Beach Resort

Pangil Beach Resort is beautifully set along the shores of Currimao in Ilocos Norte. The beach resort has an inviting tropical ambiance that blends with the place serene charm. The placid water waves and island breeze will surely gratify your stay.

Pangil Beach Resort Currimao features facilities that will surely make a lot of great memories throughout your stay. You may choose from their two dining spots the Roca Resto and the Pool Bar where you can have your favorite drinks while taking a break from dipping at the outdoor swimming pool, you may also have your kids spend their own time on water at the Kiddie Pool and play billiards with friends and family at the pool bar.
